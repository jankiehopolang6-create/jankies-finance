Our Startup Story

Born from a mission to keep students in school.

Why We Started

Every year, thousands of talented university students drop out not because of academics — but because of financial pressure. Jankie's Finance was founded by Jankie Hopolang, a former student who witnessed friends abandoning their dreams due to lack of funds.

We realized that students need more than just loans — they need a system that works with their income flow. That's why we created the 10-minute automatic withdrawal system that aligns with monthly allowances, ensuring repayment is seamless and stress-free.

Today, we've helped over 500 students stay enrolled, graduate, and build better futures. Our mission: No student should drop out because of money.
