Our Business Model

How we help students succeed while building a sustainable future.

10% Fixed Interest Loans

We charge a simple 10% interest on all student loans — no compound interest, no hidden fees. This keeps our loans affordable while ensuring sustainability.

10-Minute Auto-Withdrawal System

Our proprietary technology links to student bank accounts and automatically withdraws repayments exactly 10 minutes after monthly allowances arrive. This eliminates missed payments and late fees.

Sustainable Growth Model

We reinvest 70% of profits into helping more students. Every loan repaid funds another student's education. This creates a self-sustaining cycle of support.

Partnership Network

We partner with universities, bursary programs, and employers to ensure students have access to our services and seamless repayment integration.
